Why It Matters

Natural is Always Better

Here's what you're avoiding when you choose Roots & Grains

🫙
Wood-Pressed vs Refined Oils
Refined oils are extracted using heat and chemicals, destroying natural nutrients, Vitamin E, and flavour. Wood-pressed oils use only mechanical pressure — no heat, no chemicals, nothing lost.
Refined oil — heated to 200°C → Cold-pressed — full nutrition
🌾
Unpolished vs Polished Millets
Polishing removes the bran layer — where most fibre, minerals and B-vitamins live. Unpolished millets retain everything nature put in them. More nutrition per grain, every single time.
Polished — 60% nutrients lost → Unpolished — whole grain goodness
🧈
Natural Ghee vs Commercial Ghee
Commercial ghee is often made with added synthetic flavour and colour to mimic the real thing. Our desi ghee uses the traditional bilona method — slow, honest, and completely pure.
Commercial — artificial additives → Bilona method — 100% natural
🚜
Farm-Direct vs Store-Bought
Supermarket products pass through 4–6 middlemen before reaching you, adding weeks of storage and preservatives. We source directly and deliver fresh — from the farm to your door in days, not months.
Store-bought — months in storage → Farm-direct — days fresh
